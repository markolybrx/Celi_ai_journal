User Data and Privacy Statement

1. Introduction and Scope

Celi ("The Application," "The Service," "We") is a digital journaling companion powered by artificial intelligence. We recognize that the data entrusted to us—your personal thoughts, emotions, and memories—is sensitive and sacred. This Privacy Policy outlines the legal and technical framework governing the collection, processing, storage, and protection of User Data ("The Signal"). By registering an account, you ("The User") consent to the practices described herein.

2. Data Collection

We adhere to a principle of Data Minimization, collecting only the information strictly necessary to provide the functional services of the Application.

2.1. Identity Data

  • Username: Used as your unique identifier within the system.
  • Authentication Credentials: We store a cryptographic hash of your password. We do not store raw passwords.
  • Biographical Markers: Date of Birth is collected solely for the purpose of generating context-aware AI responses (e.g., astrological or age-relevant psychological framing).

2.2. Functional Data

  • Preferences: Interface customization settings (e.g., Favorite Color) to personalize the User experience.
  • System Logs: Timestamps of entries and interactions for sorting and retrieval purposes.

3. Journal and Collected Information (User Content)

3.1. Definition

"User Content" refers to all text entries, mood selections, and dialogue inputs submitted by the User to Celi.

3.2. Ownership

The User retains 100% Intellectual Property ownership of their User Content. Celi claims no ownership rights over your personal entries. We act solely as a custodian and processor of this data.

3.3. Usage

User Content is accessed by the Application solely for:

  • Displaying your history in the "Vault" or "Journal" view.
  • Providing context to the AI Model to generate relevant responses.
  • Generating "Soul Analysis" summaries.

4. AI Utilisation (Artificial Intelligence)

Celi leverages Google Gemini (Generative AI) to provide conversational capabilities and psychological analysis.

4.1. Data Transmission

When you submit an entry, the text is transmitted securely via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) to the Google Gemini API.

4.2. Ephemeral Processing

The AI processing is stateless and ephemeral. This means:

  • Your data is sent to the AI model for the sole purpose of generating a specific response.
  • Once the response is generated, the AI provider discards the context.

4.3. No Training on User Data

Crucial Disclaimer: Celi does not grant Google or any third-party AI provider the right to use your User Content to train their public foundation models. Your personal journals will not become part of the public AI knowledge base.

5. Data Storage & Infrastructure

5.1. Hosting

All User Data is stored in MongoDB Atlas, an enterprise-grade cloud database provider.

5.2. Encryption

  • At Rest: Data stored on our servers is encrypted using the AES-256 standard.
  • In Transit: All data moving between your device, our servers, and the AI provider is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS).

6. User Data Rights

In alignment with global privacy standards (including principles from GDPR and CCPA), you possess the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You may view your complete data history at any time via the Application's "Journal" or "Vault" interface.
  • Right to Erasure ("The Right to be Forgotten"): You have the absolute right to delete your account. This function is available in the Profile Settings via the "Delete Signal" button. Executing this action triggers an immediate, irreversible database deletion command.

7. User Data Security

7.1. Access Control

Access to your account is protected by your password. We employ Identity Verification protocols (Secret Questions) to verify ownership before allowing password resets.

7.2. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that compromises personal data, we will notify affected Users within 72 hours of discovery, outlining the nature of the breach and the remedial actions taken.
